Personalized Financial Advisor

The field of finance can be complex and overwhelming for individuals seeking personalised financial advice. In order to make informed decisions regarding investments, retirement planning, budgeting, and financial products, individuals often require guidance from financial experts. The aim of this project is to develop an Intelligent Financial Advisor powered by Machine learning models and Large Language Models (LLM) to provide personalised financial advice and guidance to individuals. By leveraging NLP and machine learning techniques, the Intelligent Financial Advisor will assist users in making informed financial decisions and achieving their financial goals. The primary objective of an AI-driven financial advisor is to help individuals and organisations make informed financial decisions, enabling real-time decision-making and continuous monitoring of financial markets.

AI Predictor

This page is an interesting mixture of AI and required tools.
  • Tool: Auto Ticker selector helps you find your desired ticker by just typing two letters.
  • Tool: News aggregator automatically fetches 9 latest news for the company selected in ticker from google news.
  • AI: Our distilBERT based sentiment analysis tool just checks the sentiment of all headlines and shows for ease of user access.
  • Tool: Sentiment Gauge is just the numerical average of sentiments presented in a graphical format for understanding overall sentimet related to company in question based on collected news.
  • AI: Future Predictor, is an interesting mix of XGBoost and live data fetched from NSE. it is trained on 80 companies 10 year data to predict next 10 days of movement of given stock, based on past data fetched live by our tool.
